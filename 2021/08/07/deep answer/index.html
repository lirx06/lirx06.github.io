Deep Web Answers

Q. Aliens?

外星人？

A. Yes. On the moon Europa, there are gigantic squid-like things. Carbon-based, like us. However, we have not made contact with anything technologically advanced, yet.

是的，在木卫二上，有着巨大的一种碳基生物，他们长得像是乌贼。然而，我们尚未与任何技术先进的人员取得联系。

Q. Tesla Experiment Plans?

特斯拉实验计划？

A. Copper wire alternating with iron bits works better than fiber optics for data transfer until a certain point. Also, it is much easier to make. Its shape is similar to that of a chain, but a certain difference. It is hidden, because it could pose a threat to the oil industry.

在数据传输方面，在一定的情况下，铜线和铁屑的交替使用会比光纤更有效。并且，这样的传输线路也更容易制作。它的形状类似于链的形状，但有一定的区别。 这种传输方式由于可能会对石油工业产生威胁而被隐藏。

Q. Nibiru? Planet X? Comet Elenin?

关于Nibiru行星，X行星，叶列宁彗星呢？

A. There is a distinct possibility that there is a brown dwarf star in the Oort Cloud. However, stories of Nibiru and Planet X are fake. Comet Elenin is nothing special.

在奥尔特云中很可能存在一个棕色的矮星。但是，关于Nibiru行星和X行星都是假的，叶列宁彗星没有什么特殊的。

Q. Josef Mengele WW2 Experiment Successes?

可以说一说关于约瑟夫·门格勒（Josef Mengele）在二战的成功试验吗？

A. Josef Mengele was a Nazi scientist during World War II who did several experiments. He was a stereotypical mad scientist. In one experiment, he put one twin in one room and the other a mile away. He then raped one of the twins. The other twin immediately started to cry. He also found a way to dye the human iris any color without permanent damage. He tested it on 200,000 human eyes.

约瑟夫·门格勒（Josef Mengele）是第二次世界大战期间的纳粹科学家，曾做过几次实验。 他是一个古怪的疯狂科学家。 在一个实验中，他将一个双胞胎放在一个房间里，另一个放在一英里外。 随后，他强奸了双胞胎中的其中一个。 另一对双胞胎立即开始哭泣。 他还找到了一种在不造成永久性损害的情况下将人类虹膜染成任何颜色的方法。 他在200,000人眼上进行了测试。

Q. Atlantis?

有没有关于亚特兰蒂斯的信息呢？

A. The real location of the lost city of Atlantis is ten miles east of the midpoint between La Esperanza and Jotajana. It is a delta now, but used to be a grass plain not long ago. The civilization there was very advanced (for that time), as they had broken from the Inca and had thick glasses and electric lights. It “sank into the ocean” after a high-magnitude ocean quake that created a large tidal wave. The Romans actually sailed to it, meaning that they reached America before Columbus; it is hypothesized that the Antikythera mechanism originated from that area.

失落的亚特兰蒂斯市的真实位置在La Esperanza和Jotajana之间的中点以东十英里处。 现在是三角洲，但不久前曾经是草场。 那里的文明非常先进，因为它们已经脱离了印加，戴着厚实的眼镜和电灯。 在发生大潮汐的强地震后，它“沉入海洋”。 罗马人实际上曾经驶向那里，这意味着他们是在哥伦布之前到达美国的。 据此推测，安提凯希拉装置起源于该地区。

Q. The Law of 13s? Illuminati? The Line of Blood?

关于光明会的13条血脉呢？

A. The Law of 13s is that there are 13 dominant bloodlines of the Illuminati, which is a gigantic family that basically have the world’s governments and big businesses under their thumb. They claim common descent from Enoch and Atlantis, though different branches shift in and out of power. The governments only begrudgingly report to the Illuminati, but they do it anyway.

13世纪法则指的是，光明会（Illuminati）有13条主要血统，这是一个庞大的家庭，基本上掌握了世界各国政府和大企业的经验。他们声称是来自伊诺克和亚特兰蒂斯的共同后裔，尽管不同的分支在权力上有出入。政府很不愿意向光明会报告，但无论如何他们不得不这么做。

The Line of Blood is a program the Illuminati makes the United States government run to track the members of its extensive family. Every ruler in all of history has had descendants, which are logged by the United States government for the Illuminati. They nearly know the lineage of every ruling power since Alexander the Great. The bloodline has spread to such an amount that there is a 1/45000 chance that anyone is on it. The database they keep it in is 180 terabytes of text, and likely includes genetic research through brute force. In this research, it is specified that humans actually consist of over 120 breeds. The diffusionist point of view is not correct; these breeds evolved independently of each other around the world. We are not just one species.

“血缘”是光明会让美国政府运行的一项计划，目的是追踪其庞大家族的成员。 历史上的每个统治者都有后裔，美国政府为了光明会记录下这些后裔。他们几乎知道自亚历山大大帝以来所有统治者的血统。血统已经蔓延到任何人都可能有1/45000的几率。他们的数据库中保存了180 T的信息，并且也许包括了通过蛮力进行的基因研究。这项研究表明人类实际上由120多个种类组成。因此，扩散主义者的观点实际是不正确的。这些种族在世界各地彼此独立地进化。我们并不归属于同一个的种族。

Q. Crystalline Power Metrics?

A. The Alexandrian lighthouse had a beam that could be seen 20 miles away. The secret behind this beam was crystalline metrics, the details of which were recorded in the Library of Alexandria. As most crystalline solids carry an electric charge, the Egyptians discovered a way to remove electricity from any electrolyte and quartz in 1300 BCE. (This technology never spread to Europe, as it was one of the last regions to become non-nomadic and were considered mostly barbarians.)

亚历山大灯塔的光束可以在20英里外看到。这个光束背后的秘密是晶体度量，其细节记录在亚历山大图书馆。由于大多数晶体固体携带电荷，埃及人在公元前1300年发现了一种从一些电解质和石英中去除电能的方法。（因为它是最后成为非游牧民族的地区之一，并且被认为是野蛮人，所以这项技术从未扩展到欧洲。）

Back in those times, quartz wielded 12 amps of electricity. In those times, Egypt relied, solely, on its power for electric lights (as displayed in paintings of handheld light sources radiating waves) and electroplating. There are still examples of mass-scale electroplating from that time period existing today. However, a lot of quartz technology stopped working. It is an unsolved mystery as to why this occurred. Of course, quartz technology still functions today, but not as efficiently. For instance, the Baghdad battery- an example of
an Egyptian electricity generator- no longer has the power to electroplate efficiently.

在那个时代，石英使用了12安培的电力。那时，埃及完全依赖于这些能源来为电灯供电（如手持式光源的绘画中所示）和电镀。今天依旧存在那个时代大量电镀的例子。然而，现在许多石英技术停止了工作，至于为什么会这样是一个未解之谜。当然，石英技术目前仍在运作，但效率不高。例如，巴格达电池——埃及发电机的一个例子——不再具有高效电镀的能力。

Q. Magic?

关于魔法呢？

A. Magic is not real. It’s just advanced technology, literally. The Illuminati of the 4000 BCE age basically used advanced technology, mostly with acoustics (acoustical levitation, etc) to control the population. This was interpreted as magic.

魔法并不是真实的，从表面上看这只是一种先进的技术。光明会在公元前四千年就基本运用了这种先进的技术来控制人口，其中大多与声学有关。这一切被解释成魔法。

Q. Occult?

关于神秘主义呢？

A. The occult, as in Aleister Crowley, is a bunch of bunk. Anarchy as a religion, like the Abrahamic religions are essentially personal tyranny. It was essential to balance the scale. Some Illuminati bloodlines follow it in a “purer” form.

神秘主义，就像阿莱斯特·克劳利(Aleister Crowley)说的，是一堆废话。无政府主义作为一种信仰，就像亚伯拉罕的宗教本质上是个人专制。因此，保持规模的平衡至关重要。而一些光明会的血统以“更纯粹”的形式跟随这种主义。

Q. Gadolinium Gallium Garnet Quantum Electronic Processors?（GGGQEP）

关于钆镓石榴石量子电子处理器有哪些信息？

A. http://pastebin.com/ktvMzynS（其中包含的文档是GGGQEP.txt的信息，日后具体翻译。）You only need three lasers to write the data and one to read. It’s the same technology as 3D glass etching. If you double its size to 10 cm 3, then you have 8 times the storage, and even if you are not atomically precise, you could hold two internets.

你只需要三个激光器就可以进行数据写入，然后另外一个进行读取。这和3D玻璃蚀刻技术是一样的。如果你将它的尺寸变为两倍，即10 cm3，你就可以获得以前存储空间的八倍。即便你在实验过程中并不是很精准，这也仍旧足以存储两个互联网的信息。

Q. Ancient Advanced Technology?

关于古代先进的技术呢？

A. Nothing that we don’t have now. The Great Pyramids were initiation chambers for the Mysteries of Osiris, of which there is little documentation due to it being secret, like the Eleusinian Mysteries (which were based on drugs). Their important properties were acoustics. And no, nothing about psychic anything; mostly just acoustics and drugs.

没有什么是我们现在所没有的。壮观的金字塔是Osiris神秘主义的起源，由于它的保密性所以存在很少相关的文献，就像厄琉西斯密仪（Eleusinian Mysteries）（以药物为基础）。它们的重要特点是关于一些声学特性。而并不是通灵，它们其中大多都只是与声学以及药物有关。

Q. Advanced Consciousness? Paradigm Recalescence?

先进的意识形态，范式复辉。

A. Paradigm recalescence is essentially superconsciousness. A step-up from out-of-body experiences. It’s a connection to the universe (an omniscient entity) in which you are using it like an information sponge.

范式复辉本质上是一种超意识的东西。这是出体的一种进步。这将个体与宇宙（全知全能的一种东西）所联系起来，这种状态就像是在信息海绵中的状态。

Q. Animal or Plant Intelligence?

动物或者植物有自己的智力吗？

A. Very. The majority of animal and plant life have awareness equal to that of humans.

大多数动植物生命的意识与人类相同。

Q. Mythological gods?

神话中的神存在吗？

A. All ‘gods’, worshiped today or in the past, were Illuminati members in disguise to control the population. In cases of gods communicating with their worshipers for hundreds of years, the act of playing the deity is entrusted to a bloodline.

所有所谓的‘神’，无论是今天还是过去所崇拜的，都是由光明会的成员所假扮以控制人口。在几百年中，神与他们的崇拜者不断交流，在这种情况下，扮演神的行为被委托给了其中的一支血脉。

Q. Reptilians?

关于蜥蜴人呢？

A. Nope. No technologically advanced aliens of any sort have ever visited Earth.

不，没有任何种类的技术先进的外星人到达过地球。
